# Stopped For Futility

The local Gate 1 candidate-vs-r1 H2H gate was intentionally stopped after 276
decisive games, at an even side-swapped boundary. It did not complete the
preplanned 500-game gate and must not be reported as a completed promotion test.

## Result At Stop

- Candidate wins: 140
- r1 wins: 136
- Candidate win rate: 50.72%
- 95% Wilson interval: 44.86% to 56.57%
- Voids: 0
- Errors: 0

## Rationale

The candidate tracked approximately 50% throughout the local evaluation and
provided no evidence of an improvement over r1. Promotion at 500 games would
require at least 272 candidate wins (a Wilson lower bound above 50%). From the
stopping result, that would require 132 wins in the remaining 224 games
(58.93%), which was not a reasonable continuation target given the observed
run. The result is therefore recorded as stopped for futility / no evidence of
improvement, rather than a promotion or regression decision.
